Mollie

Payment company building the best payments experience in Europe. Working across onboarding, activation, and the merchant dashboard to reduce friction and increase conversion.

Role Product Designer II
Timeline 2025 – Now

Intro

Mollie is one of Europe's fastest-growing payment service providers. I joined the Onboarding & Activation (BET) team to improve the merchant journey from signup to first payment, the most critical part of the funnel.

Problem

New merchants faced a complex, multi-step onboarding flow with unclear progress, redundant verification steps, and high drop-off at key moments. The experience didn't reflect Mollie's brand promise of simplicity.

Solution

Redesigned the onboarding flow with a linear task model, milestone moments, and contextual guidance. Reduced cognitive load by splitting the flow into clearly scoped steps with visible progress.

Impact

Ongoing. Working closely with data, engineering, and product to ship iterative improvements. Tracking activation rate, time-to-first-payment, and support ticket volume as primary success metrics.

Takeaways

Working inside a unicorn with mature design systems and processes reinforced the value of constraints. Every decision needs evidence, every change needs instrumentation, and clarity of intent is the real superpower.
