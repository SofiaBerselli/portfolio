Mollie

Europe's fastest-growing payments company. I joined the Onboarding & Activation team to redesign how merchants go from signup to their first payment: the most critical and complex part of the funnel.

Role

Product Designer II

Timeline

Mollie

The problem

New merchants signed up, completed KYC, and stalled. The 30-day activation rate sat at 22% in 2024: most merchants never reached their first payment. App-started journeys converted worse than desktop. The onboarding system was built for regulatory compliance, not for activation: fragmented across web, mobile, and MBA, with no clear signal of progress or next steps after KYC.

Mollie / Takeaways

Takeaways

Designing for activation means designing for intention: not just moving merchants through steps, but helping them understand what those steps are for. Onboarding systems live at the intersection of compliance, trust, and conversion. Working across Product, Risk, and Compliance taught me that the best design decisions happen when you treat constraints as inputs, not obstacles.
