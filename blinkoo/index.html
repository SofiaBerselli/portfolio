blinkoo

Travel startup betting that short-form video would win travel discovery. I joined at founding as the only designer: product vision, interface design, and design system, from zero to launch.

Role Founding Designer
Timeline 2020 – 2022

Intro

blinkoo was a travel startup aiming to connect travellers with local hosts through authentic, short-form video experiences. I joined at founding as the first and only designer, owning the product end-to-end: vision, architecture, interface, and the design system, through to launch and growth.

Problem

Existing platforms were photo-first and algorithm-heavy. Research confirmed that 90% of consumers cite authentic peer content as the primary driver of trust, yet no travel app had fully bet its model on it. Video already accounted for 82% of all internet traffic. Gen Z and Millennials were quietly deserting traditional OTAs for exactly this gap: no honest, peer-to-peer way to gauge what an experience actually felt like before booking.

Vision

Build a platform where proof of experience is the currency. Short-form video as the primary discovery layer, browsable like a social feed, bookable like a marketplace. We defined success through three pillars:

PillarThe problemSuccess
Market traction Breaking into a competitive market 500k+ downloads in Q1
UGC quality Encouraging raw vs polished video 5K uploads, 20K interactions
Capital validation Proving the model to investors €7M+ funding secured

Gamification

To prevent engagement churn, common in travel apps where users browse but never contribute, I designed a reward system built on real-world value. Hosts earn Blinkoins for high-quality, categorised uploads, redeemable for travel credit on Airbnb, Booking.com, and TheFork. Expertise Breakdowns (Food, Accommodation, Sports) turned regular users into verified Local Guides with genuine authority in their category.

Filtering

Traditional search (city, category, scroll) creates too much distance between inspiration and decision. I led a discovery system that lets users search by activity, country, or blinkoo Looks, a curated vibe-based filter. Each result integrates direct booking links, collapsing the gap between seeing something and booking it.

Backoffice

The consumer app was only half the product. I designed the B2B backoffice to manage a massive influx of UGC: category management for thousands of videos, host analytics, booking workflows, and moderation tools. I also built and maintained the blinkoo Design System from scratch, the foundation that kept quality consistent while the product scaled fast.

Impact

blinkoo became a Top 10 Travel App in Italy, earned a 4.8-star App Store rating, and secured €7M+ in funding. The model held: users wanted raw, honest video over polished content.

Takeaways

Designing 0 to 1 at founding pace taught me that the best UX doesn't make things easier, it makes them more honest. Scope clarity matters as much as craft. And a design system isn't overhead: it's what lets you move fast without breaking things.
